Clang-Format-Web

clang-format styling in the web, completely local.

Methodology

This site contains clang-format compiled to WASM. Everytime you modify either the .clang-format or the "Code" textarea, a Javascript handler will fire, call a WASM-compiled clang-format, and format your code.
Selecting a preset will delete the contents of the .clang-format area!
